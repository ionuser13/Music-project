Videoclub

Videoclub was an electropop and synthpop french duo, which was formed in 2018. Its members were Adele Castillon and Matthieu Reynaud

This duo came to life in Nantes, on June 2018. Their first single was "Amour Plastique", which had a successful reception. The first Videoclub concert took place in Nantes, on April 11th, 2019. On summer of that year they did a musical tour.

Their fisrt collaboration happened on 9th October, 2020 with Natalia Lacunza, a spanish singer. The name of the single is "Enfance 80".

Euphories is their first album, which was published on 29th January, 2021.

After 3 years of relationship and existence, Videoclub announced its separation in their last videoclip "SMS"
