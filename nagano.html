About the interpreters

CLUBZ

CLUBZ is a duo from Monterrey, Mexico. It's formed by Orlando and Coco.

Due to the excesive violence Monterrey was going through in 2013 (and with that the low availability of places to make them themselves known), the duo opted to make themselves known through social media. They succeeded. They got to play in the most popular music festivals of their country, like Vive Latino, Nrmal and Ceremonia. They also got to play in United States, Costa Rica and Ecuador.

Popular releases:

  • Nagano
  • Popscuro
  • Áfrika

Ela Minus

Ela Minus is the pseudonym of Gabriela Jimeno Caldas, a singer, songwriter, drummer and producer of electronic and techno-pop music. She is from Colombia, but she lives in United States.

Her music career started when she was 12, being the drummer of a hardcore rock band, "Ratón Perez" for about 10 years. The "Ela Minus" idea started to appear when she moved to Brooklyn, where DIY culture, clubs' scenes and synthesizers inspired her. Before starting it, she was part of a punk band, "Balancer" and a drummer in various bands' tours.

Until 2019 she used to publish her music independently, when Domino Records signed with her so she can launch her album "Acts of Rebellion".
